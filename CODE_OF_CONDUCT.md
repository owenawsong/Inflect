# Code of Conduct

Inflect follows a simple standard: be useful, specific, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- critique ideas and results, not people
- report bugs with enough detail to reproduce them
- be honest about uncertainty
- respect voice, dataset, and model licensing boundaries
- avoid hype claims that are not supported by evidence

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- sharing private audio or credentials
- encouraging non-consensual voice cloning
- spamming unrelated model promotion
- misrepresenting generated samples or benchmark results

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who repeatedly ignore these rules.
